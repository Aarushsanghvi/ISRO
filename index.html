ISRO – Indian Space Research Organisation

Founded in 1969, ISRO is India's national space agency known for its cost-effective and high-impact space missions. From launching satellites to exploring the Moon and Mars, ISRO continues to push boundaries.

Major Missions

  • Chandrayaan-3: India’s third lunar mission, made a soft landing on the Moon’s south pole.
  • Mangalyaan: The Mars Orbiter Mission – first Asian mission to Mars, achieved in its first attempt.
  • Aditya-L1: India’s first solar observatory to study the Sun from Lagrange Point 1.

Why ISRO Matters

ISRO plays a critical role in national development through satellite communication, Earth observation, and navigation. It also inspires millions with its space exploration feats done on a tight budget.
